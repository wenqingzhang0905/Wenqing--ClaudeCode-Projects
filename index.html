About Me

Intentional about every step

I didn't fall into sustainability — I chose it. After years at the IMF and World Bank translating macro data into policy, and an MBA at MIT Sloan building fluency in capital and innovation, I joined climate tech to do the work that actually moves the needle.

What I've learned: the biggest gap in climate action isn't ideas or technology — it's the bridge between intention and measurable impact. That's where I work. Where financial modeling meets carbon accountability. Where strategy meets ground truth.

I also write about it — on Substack in English and 小红书 in Chinese — because the conversation needs more people in it.
