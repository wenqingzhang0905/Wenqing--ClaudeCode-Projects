About Me

Intentional about every step

I didn't fall into sustainability — I chose it. After years at the IMF and World Bank translating macro data into policy, and an MBA at MIT Sloan, I joined climate tech to do the work that actually moves the needle. The biggest gap in climate action isn't ideas — it's the bridge between intention and measurable impact. That's where I build.

I also write about it on Substack (English) and 小红书 (Chinese), because this conversation needs more people in it.
